### - **Tuple:** (1, 'span', 4, 'U'), tuple('span'), namedtuple
### - **Dictionary:** {'key1': 'value1', 'key2': 'value2'}
### - **Set:** {1, 2, 3, 4, 5}, set([1, 2, 3, 4, 5])
### - **File:** open('eggs.txt')
### - **Boolean:** True, False
### - **None:** None
### - **Functions, modules, classes**
### - **Advanced:** Decorators, Generators, Iterators, MetaProgramming
